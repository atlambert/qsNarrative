The Quantified Self

Community & Context

Quantified Self (n).

externalizing our bodies through data to learn more about ourselves.

This process creates a bridge between our biological and personal information and the technical products in our lives.

Quantified Self is also a broader tech trend that has grown as sensors have become cheaper & more ubiquitous

The quantified self is more than just tools, though; it's about starting with a question

The first quantified self meetup took place in November 2008 in Kevin Kelley's basement. 28 people were in attendance.

And ever since, the community has grown across the world

The quantified self represents a diverse number of viewpoints on its aims

Some in the community want it to be more rigorous and academic; others believe in a more casual approach.

In the end, it's probably beneficial that people are thinking critically and asking questions about data, regardless of background
